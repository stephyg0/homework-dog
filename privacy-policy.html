Privacy Policy for Homework Hound

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

Homework Hound respects your privacy. This extension is designed purely for entertainment purposes and does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Information Collection

Homework Hound does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Account information
  • Passwords or authentication data
  • Financial information
  • Location data
  • Browsing history
  • Analytics or tracking data

The extension may temporarily access webpage content only to display the pixel dog animation and generate screenshots requested by the user. This content is processed locally on the user's device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Screenshots and Downloads

If the user chooses to save an "evidence" screenshot, the image is generated locally and downloaded directly to the user's device. Homework Hound does not upload or store screenshots.

Remote Code

Homework Hound does not use remote code. All scripts and assets are packaged within the extension.

Third-Party Services

Homework Hound does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or external tracking tools.

Data Storage

Any temporary extension settings, such as animation preferences, are stored locally within the browser using Chrome extension storage APIs.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future if the extension's functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
